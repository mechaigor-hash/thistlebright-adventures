Thistlebright Adventures — Image-Heavy Bestiary

Print note: This A4 edition uses layered parchment panels. Artwork is anchored to its text block so race/class portraits and scene art should not split across printed pages.

Every creature has a dedicated picture, a kid-safe role, and at least three non-combat ways to interact.

Scottish Fairy Guide — Ailsa Thistledown

  • Mood: Sparkly, bossy in a friendly way.
  • Wants: Brave children to help fix fairy mishaps.
  • Gentle Trouble: Gives too many clues at once.
  • How to Help: Ask her to slow down and draw a map.
  • Game Note: Can appear when players are stuck.

Three gentle solutions

  • Kind: Offer comfort, share something, or ask what it needs.
  • Clever: Notice a clue, solve a pattern, or remember a fairy tale.
  • Brave/Quick: Protect a friend, climb carefully, race, or distract with silliness.
