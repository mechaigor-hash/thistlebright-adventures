# Artwork Prompt 06 — Guide Token Cards and Cozy Table Scene

**Purpose:** Printable visual support for the Dungeon Master Guide: a cozy tabletop RPG scene for ages 5–7 with simple token cards for Brave, Clever, Kind, Quick, charms, lantern safety word, and tiny treasure rewards.

**Prompt:**
A warm storybook watercolor illustration for a child-friendly tabletop RPG called “Thistlebright Adventures.” Show a cozy wooden table in a Scottish fairy cottage, with printable game cards and tokens laid out neatly: four large friendly stat cards labeled Brave, Clever, Kind, Quick; three glowing charm tokens; a small paper lantern card for the safety word; tartan ribbons, thistles, heather sprigs, rowan berries, a tiny wooden shield, pebble wand, shell, and moon-muffin. In the background, a friendly Scottish fairy guide, a brownie with flour on their nose, and a gentle kelpie peeking from a window beside a moonlit loch. High fantasy wonder, soft golden lantern light, rounded child-safe shapes, no horror, no weapons except toy-like wooden props, no danger, no text except the clear card labels, printable composition with clean margins, consistent with whimsical watercolor children’s book art.

**Negative prompt:** gore, horror, scary monster, dark grim tone, realistic violence, sharp weapons, frightening expressions, cluttered unreadable text, photorealism.

**Aspect ratio:** landscape
