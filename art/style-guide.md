# Artwork Style Guide

Consistent visual direction for all images:
- Cozy high fantasy storybook illustration.
- Scottish fairy myth details: thistles, heather, tartan ribbons, standing stones, lochs, rowan trees, mossy castles.
- Age 5–7 friendly: rounded shapes, warm expressions, no gore, no horror, no sharp menace.
- Palette: heather purple, moss green, warm gold, misty blue, soft cream.
- Lighting: magical dawn/dusk glow, firefly sparkles.
- Text: avoid readable text inside generated art unless specifically requested.
